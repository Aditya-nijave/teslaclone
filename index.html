A Spacious Interior Built for Every Passenger

Model X offers a spacious cabin with the world's largest panoramic windshield and seating for up to seven. Front doors open and close automatically, with Falcon Wing rear doors that make loading passengers and cargo easier than ever.

  • 17" cinematic display with 2200 x 1300 resolution
  • Tri-zone temperature controls
  • HEPA air filtration system
  • Up to 88 cubic feet of cargo space
  • Premium audio system with 22 speakers

Unmatched Performance

Model X Plaid is the highest performing SUV ever built, with 1,020 horsepower generated by three high-performance motors with carbon-sleeved rotors.

  • 0-60 mph in 2.5 seconds
  • 1,020 horsepower
  • 9.9 seconds quarter mile
  • 175 mph top speed
  • Tri Motor All-Wheel Drive
  • Torque vectoring during cornering
